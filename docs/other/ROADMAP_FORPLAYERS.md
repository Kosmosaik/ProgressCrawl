# Player-Facing Roadmap (Non-Technical Summary)

> **NOTE:** This file is an optional player-facing summary. The authoritative roadmap is `docs/ROADMAP.md`.  
> If `docs/ROADMAP.md` contains a Player-Facing section, keep this file in sync or mark it deprecated.

 

##### This section explains upcoming updates in gameplay terms. It mirrors the technical roadmap but avoids implementation details

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M0 — Foundations & Stability (ACTIVE)

- More reliable saves and loading. (FINISHED)
- Cleaner internal architecture to prevent future bugs.
- Interactions behave more consistently.
- The groundwork for all future systems is reinforced.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M1 — Better Zone UI & Usability

- Camera/viewport system so large zones run smoothly.
- UI panels that stay on-screen.
- Improved zone rendering performance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M2 — More Interesting Exploration (Locations & POIs)

- Enterable mini-locations inside zones.
- A new biome with its own terrain and spawns.
- More meaningful exploration structure.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M3 — Difficulty That Actually Matters

- Harder areas become rarer and more rewarding.
- Enemies and resources scale more clearly with difficulty.
- Higher risk = higher reward.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M3.25 — Quality Grades & Better Results (Not More Loot)

- Creatures, nodes, and stashes spawn with a quality grade.
- Your tools and skills determine how much quality you extract.
- Higher quality means better materials and stronger crafted items.
- Old zones can become valuable again after refresh cycles.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M4 — Actions Feel Like Real Time

- Unified timing system for all actions.
- Day/Night cycle.
- Seasonal progression.
- Clear action messages while harvesting, crafting, traveling, etc.
- Zone refresh timers begin working.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M5 — Survival Begins

- Hunger and thirst become real pressures.
- You must eat and drink to survive.
- Stamina affects what you can do.
- HP regeneration depends on survival condition.
- Survival is meaningful but not punishing.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M6 — Hands, Tools & Real Item Use

- Equip items in left/right hands.
- Use tools directly on the world.
- Combine items for simple crafting.
- Looting becomes interaction-based (no global loot button).
- Durability matters.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M7 — Carrying & Encumbrance

- Start limited — hands-only carry.
- Backpacks unlock larger inventory.
- Weight affects stamina and movement.
- STR affects carrying capacity.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M8 — Gathering Becomes a System

- Harvesting takes time.
- Tools and skills matter.
- Nodes regenerate after refresh.
- Gathering skills influence quality outcomes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M9 — Processing Animals & Materials

- Skinning and butchering require deliberate actions.
- Multi-step sinew processing chain.
- Different actions yield different materials.
- Corpse interface allows multiple processing choices.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M10 — Primitive Crafting & Shelter

- Field crafting and station crafting.
- Fire and camp interactions.
- Lean-to and shelter placement.
- Quality of materials affects crafting results.
- Gather → Craft → Survive loop becomes functional.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M11 — A Living World (Before Combat)

- Creatures roam.
- Some flee, some defend territory.
- Zones refresh after full exploration.
- Entities reroll per refresh cycle.
- The world feels alive even before combat.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M12 — Death = Setbacks (Not Permadeath)

- Inventory loss (partial/full).
- Durability damage.
- Respawn rules.
- Progress continues — but with consequences.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M12.5 — Levels & Character Growth

- Gain XP from survival actions and exploration.
- Allocate attribute points.
- Skills grow over time.
- Long-term progression begins.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M13 — Combat (Layered on Survival)

- Combat UI transition.
- Auto-attack system.
- Engage / Defend / Flee.
- Victory screen with corpse processing.
- XP from defeating creatures.
- Combat supports survival — not the other way around.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M14 — Quests & Tutorial

- Guided tutorial zone.
- Tasks required to exit tutorial.
- Later: optional world quests.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## M15 — Economy

- Vendors.
- Buy/Sell or barter.
- More reasons to gather and craft.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
